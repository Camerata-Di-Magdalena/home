The Holy Grill

The Holy Grill of St. Nicholas brings the basic needs of physical and spiritual nourishment, as well as that of fellowship to the homeless where they are. A ray of light and hope into what can otherwise be a dark existence.
We’ve now served nearly 60,000 hot, spirit-lifting meals and we’ve expanded our ministry to serve food insecure families with children (100 meals in just a couple of hours on a recent Saturday). To meet the increasing need, we recently announced our plans to purchase for $150,000 a custom-equipped food truck that will double our cooking capacity.
