Introduction

Camerata Di Magdalena is a nonprofit 501C3 volunteer organization which raises money for feeding the homeless of South Florida through the Holy Grill of St. Nicholas. They are a unique student group under the guidance of Eric Berken with a focus on the practice and performance of music from the Baroque era.
